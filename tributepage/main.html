Tribute to Shahrukh Khan

Shahrukh Khan, also known as the "King of Bollywood", has captivated audiences with his charm, versatility, and powerful performances for decades. Here are some key highlights of his illustrious career.

Career Highlights

  • 1992: Debut in Bollywood with "Deewana".
  • 1995: "Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge" becomes a blockbuster.
  • 1998: Wins Filmfare Award for Best Actor for "Dil Se".
  • 2002: Stars in "Devdas", receiving international acclaim.
  • 2010: "My Name is Khan" gains global recognition.
  • 2017: Receives the Crystal Award at the World Economic Forum.
